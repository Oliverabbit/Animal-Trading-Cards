The African chameleon

Chameleon has a multilayer skin which allows it to change the color of the skin as a way of expressing her mood and to defend itself.

  • Species: C.africanus
  • Average Length: 35-45 cm
  • Lifespan: 5 years
  • Family: Chamaeleonidea

The African chameleon is a species of chameleon native to the Sahel and The Nile Valley, the average size is between 34 cm to 45cm long. It feeds mainly on insects which it catches by suddenly extending its sticky-tipped tongue. lizards or fledgling birds can also be eaten by the chameleon.
